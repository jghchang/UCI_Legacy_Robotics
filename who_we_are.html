About the Team

Legacy Robotics launched its Mars Rover initiative to bring together a strong community of passionate and ambitious undergraduate students, committed to advancing space exploration technology. Our team focuses on the entire development process, from designing to building, ultimately aiming to compete at the University Rover Challenge. This quarter, a team of 35 students work collaboratively to push the boundaries of robotics, precision engineering, and autonomous systems as we take on challenges.
