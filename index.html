Our Mission

Legacy Robotics is a student-run team promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through building robots. We aim to inspire innovation and team work through cutting-edge technology, fostering hands-on engineering experience and practical projects as well as cultivate passion among students.

Our Goals
  1. Fabricate a fully-functional rover by Spring 2025.
  2. Iterate and improve on design in time for the Preliminary Design Review (PDR) 2025.
  3. Submit our rover for the Systems Acceptance Review (SAR) and compete in the University Rover Challenge 2026!
